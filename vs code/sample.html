LENDING

FinnOne Neo is the next-generation lending solution built on an advanced technology platform, designed to shape the future of lending across Retail, Corporate and Islamic sectors for banks and financial service companies. The multi-channel solution which helps digitize the complete loan lifecycle end to end, supports both cloud and on-premise deployments. Cloud deployment provides flexibility in scale while optimizing costs by supporting pay-per-use models.
